Think of this as a partnership. You go through a proven 5-step process. I am the person making sure you complete it, holding you to the highest standard, and providing you with the tools, resources, and access to my network when needed.

What's the investment?

That's a conversation we have together once we've established that we're a good fit. But I'll say this. The people who lead with that question are often the same people still asking it five years from now.

High-level founders don't think about investment the way employees do. They think about return. They think about allocation of resources. They think about risk and what it costs them to stay exactly where they are.

Every single person who has worked with me and done the work has made their investment back and then some. There are multiple options available — the right person always finds a way.

How long do clients typically work with you?

Anywhere from 12 weeks to multiple years. I have clients who have been with me for three, four, five years and beyond. Those are the people I'm ultimately looking for. Not someone who wants a quick fix and disappears — someone who understands that building something real takes time.

It's non-exclusive. You can still be part of other masterminds, other groups, other communities. What it means is that you have someone in your corner who knows your business, knows your history, and is consistently invested in your growth over the long term.

What kind of support do I actually get?

Direct access on WhatsApp. Complete access to my calendar. When something comes up, we deal with it. There's no middleman, no ticketing system, no waiting.

How often we talk is entirely up to you and where you're at. Some clients are in my WhatsApp consistently. Others surface every few weeks or months with something specific to work through. Both are fine.

The goal was never for you to need me forever. It was always to make you someone who doesn't.
